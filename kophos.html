KOPHOS

  • A clip will be played showcasing a technological scene from a movie. The contestants have to figure out the kind of technology depicted in that particular scene.

  • Interested people can participate in a team of 2.

  • Participants can be from same or different colleges or branches.

  • Use of mobile phones during competition are strictly prohibited.

  • Each team should bring their own laptop.

  • The decision of judges is final.
