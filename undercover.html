UNDERCOVER

DESCRIPTION:

  • It is a word based game. It is an individual player game which has 3 characters namely Civilian, Undercover and Mr.White.
  • The Civilians and Undercover characters only have a secret word. The secret word is similar for Civilians and Undercover but they were not same word.
  • Mr.White does not have any word. But they have to guess the civilian word.
  • Your goal is to find out the other playerâ€™s identities (and yours) as fast as possible to eliminate your enemies.
  • Remember to keep your secret word hidden from others.

OBJECTIVES:

  • Civilians win if they eliminate all Undercovers and Mr.Whites.
  • The Imposters (Undercovers and Mr.Whites) win if they survive until only 1 Civilian is left.
  • Mr.White also wins if they guess the Civilians word correctly.
