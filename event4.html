QUIZZY BUZZERS

Event Details:

  • • Maximum of 2 participants per team.
  • • Quiz will be based on technical questions related to a specific topic announced at the start.
  • Three rounds:

    • Paper and Pen round: Teams given a set of questions to answer in a time frame.
    • Buzzer round: Questions asked, first team to buzz in gets to answer.
    • Rapid-Fire round: Series of rapid-fire questions, teams must answer quickly.
  • • After each round, scores are tallied and the team with the lowest score is eliminated.
  • • This elimination process continues until only 1 team remains.
  • • Quizzy burzzers decisions are final and binding.
  • • Marks scored in each round are final, no requests for revaluation will be entertained.
